At PAAKTO, privacy and community data protection are fundamental. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the information obtained through our website and in our participatory design workshops and citizen consultations.

### 1. Data Collection
We collect information only when it is voluntarily provided, such as when filling out our contact form or signing up for a community workshop. This information may include:
- Full name.
- Email address.
- District or neighborhood of residence (for geographic analysis and urban context).

### 2. Data Use
The collected information is used exclusively to:
- Communicate details and invitations to co-design workshops and community assemblies.
- Process and structure community proposals for heat mitigation.
- Conduct aggregated demographic studies regarding urban needs in response to climate change.

### 3. Data Protection
We implement technical security measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. We do not share personally identifiable data with third parties for commercial or advertising purposes.
